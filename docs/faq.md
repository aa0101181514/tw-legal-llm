# FAQ

### Do I need a paid Claude / Cursor subscription?

You need whatever subscription gives your AI client (Claude Desktop, Cursor,
etc.) the ability to call MCP tools. TLL itself does not call any LLM —
your LLM does the generation.

### Will it work with ChatGPT?

Not yet. ChatGPT Plus users can use Custom GPT Actions, which is on our
roadmap. For now: Claude Desktop, Cursor, Cline, and other MCP-compatible
clients.

### Why aren't there raw scores in results?

Scores can be misleading and are easy to misinterpret. We return ranks
instead.

### Can I retrieve any judgment by doc_id without searching first?

No. `get_judgment_fulltext` requires the `result_token` issued by a recent
`search_judgments` call. This prevents bulk scraping.

### Can I search judgments older than X years?

The dataset covers Taiwan court judgments published over multiple decades.
Specific coverage details are not published.

### Is the dataset complete?

We index publicly available Taiwan court judgments. Coverage and freshness
are not guaranteed; see the [judgment page](https://dr-lawbot.com) for known
limitations.

### Is the answer my AI gives me legally accurate?

No. The answer is generated by your LLM client, not by us. We provide the
citations; legal interpretation is up to your LLM (and you). Always verify
critical citations on the linked `dr-lawbot.com` page.

### Can I see the backend source code?

No. The retrieval pipeline is proprietary. This client is open source; the
backend is not.

### Can I self-host?

No. TLL is a hosted service. The MCP client in this repo only proxies to
`tll.dr-lawbot.com`.
